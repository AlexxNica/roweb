Reproducible research
Generate machine readable metadata for any tabular data (EML) or phylogenetic trees and instantly upload the data to permanent repositories like figshare, DataONE, Zenodo. Automatically insert git hashes into research papers, cache your data with dat, and track provenance of your results.
